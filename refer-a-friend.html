Refer a Friend

Sharing is caring! Help your friends unlock financial opportunities by referring them to Credify. Our referral program rewards both you and your friends for joining our community and making smarter financial decisions together.

How It Works

  • Invite your friends to sign up for Credify using your unique referral link.

  • There’s no limit – the more friends you refer, the more benefits you receive!

Get Started
